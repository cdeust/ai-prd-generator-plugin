| ID | Requirement | Target | Measurement |
|----|-------------|--------|-------------|
| NFR-001 | Policy hierarchy resolution (4-level merge) completes within latency target | < 5ms at p95 | Iteration-based benchmark: 100 iterations, sort by duration, index at position 95 |

| NFR-005 | Domain-layer policy types contain no platform framework imports | Zero imports beyond Foundation | Static analysis: grep for UIKit/SwiftUI/AppKit imports in `Domain/` |
| NFR-006 | Policy composition adds negligible overhead to total prompt construction time | < 1% of total prompt construction time | Benchmark: measure prompt construction with and without policy; delta < 1% |
